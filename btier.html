📈 Core System

The game runs entirely on passive A gain. You begin earning 1 A per second automatically. All upgrades improve or multiply that passive gain.

⚙️ Upgrades Overview

  • Upgrade 1: Multiplies your passive A/sec by (2 + 0.1 × Upgrade 3 count) each purchase. This is your main scaling path.
  • Upgrade 2: Adds +10 A/sec flat. Unlocked at B-tier 2.
  • Upgrade 3: Adds +0.1 to Upgrade 1’s base multiplier permanently. Unlocked at B-tier 3.

💎 B-tier Resets

  • B-tier 1: Unlocks resetting for permanent progression (×3 passive gain multiplier).
  • B-tier 2: Unlocks Upgrade 2 (+10 A/sec).
  • B-tier 3: Unlocks Upgrade 3 (+0.1 Upgrade 1 Base Power).
  • B-tier 4 and beyond: Each new B-tier multiplies your total passive gain by ×3.

🧮 Reset Scaling

Each reset requires reaching a threshold of A before it becomes available. The threshold scales roughly by ×6 per B-tier (e.g., 500 → 3 000 → 18 000, etc.).

💡 Strategy Tips

  • Upgrade 1 is your long-term engine — stack it whenever possible.
  • Upgrade 3 permanently boosts future Upgrade 1 efficiency.
  • Reset often once you reach the B-tier threshold to accelerate growth.
  • Upgrade 2 gives stability early, but Upgrade 1 + 3 combos scale exponentially later.
